- PASS: evidence supports the requirement.

- BLOCKED: source-basis safety, environment, scope, or prior-change conflicts prevent completion.
- PARTIAL: evidence is incomplete.

- ENVIRONMENT BLOCKED: the intended runtime command cannot execute due to the local environment.
- FAIL: evidence contradicts the requirement.
